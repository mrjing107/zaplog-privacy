感谢您使用 ZapLog 无感记账（以下简称"ZapLog"或"我们"）。我们非常重视您的个人信息和隐私保护。本隐私政策旨在向您说明我们如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的信息。

一、信息收集

照片访问

ZapLog 需要访问您的照片库（Photo Library），以便读取支付截图进行 OCR 文字识别。我们仅在您主动打开 App 时访问照片。

我们不会上传您的任何照片到服务器。照片仅用于设备本地的文字识别，识别完成后立即释放内存。

OCR 文字识别

截图中的文字识别（金额、商户名称等）使用 Apple Vision Framework 在您的设备本地完成。识别过程完全离线进行，不会发送到任何外部服务器。

我们不收集什么

  • 我们不收集您的姓名、邮箱、电话号码
  • 我们不收集您的位置信息
  • 我们不使用任何第三方分析或追踪 SDK
  • 我们不使用任何广告网络

二、信息使用

我们收集的信息仅用于以下目的：

  • 识别截图中的金额和商户信息
  • 生成您的个人支出记录
  • 提供月度/年度统计图表
  • 在您主动触发时导出 CSV 数据

三、信息存储

  • 支出数据：以加密 JSON 文件形式存储在您设备的本地 Documents 目录
  • 设置信息：存储在 iOS UserDefaults（本地，不上传）
  • 商户学习数据：存储在 iOS UserDefaults（本地，不上传）
  • 我们不会将您的任何个人信息发送到外部服务器
  • 您可随时在 App 内删除所有数据

四、信息共享

我们不会与任何第三方共享、出售或出租您的个人信息。

五、广告追踪

ZapLog 不包含任何第三方广告 SDK，不使用任何行为追踪工具。

六、您的权利

您对您的数据拥有完全控制权：

  • 删除所有数据：App 设置页面 → 删除所有记录
  • 撤销照片权限：iOS 设置 → ZapLog → 照片 → 关闭
  • 导出数据：App 设置页面 → 导出 CSV
  • 查看数据：App 主界面直接查看

七、儿童隐私

ZapLog 不面向13岁以下的儿童，也不收集儿童的个人信息。

八、政策更新

如我们对本隐私政策进行重大更新，会在 App 内显著位置提示。请定期查阅。

九、联系我们

如您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过以下方式联系我们：

  • 邮箱：zaplogapp@icloud.com
  • App Store 反馈：TestFlight 或 App Store 评价页

十、法律适用

ZapLog · Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 4, 2026

App: ZapLog / 无感记账

Developer: Hao Gu

Contact Email: zaplogapp@icloud.com

Thank you for using ZapLog ("ZapLog" or "we"). We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

1. Information Collection

Photo Library Access

ZapLog accesses your Photo Library to read payment screenshots for OCR text recognition. We only access photos when you actively open the App.

We never upload your photos to any server. Photos are used solely for on-device text recognition and are released from memory immediately after processing.

OCR Text Recognition

Text recognition (amounts, merchant names, etc.) from screenshots is performed locally on your device using Apple's Vision Framework. The recognition process is entirely offline and is never sent to any external server.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or phone number
  • We do not collect your location information
  • We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not use any advertising networks

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Recognize amounts and merchant information from screenshots
  • Generate your personal expense records
  • Provide monthly and yearly statistics and charts
  • Export data to CSV when you actively trigger it

3. Data Storage

  • Expense data: Stored as encrypted JSON files in your device's local Documents directory
  • Settings: Stored in iOS UserDefaults (local only, never uploaded)
  • Merchant learning data: Stored in iOS UserDefaults (local only, never uploaded)
  • We never send any personal information to external servers
  • You can delete all data at any time from within the App

4. Information Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to any third party.

5. Advertising & Tracking

ZapLog does not include any third-party advertising SDKs and does not use any behavioral tracking tools.

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete all data: Settings → Delete All Records
  • Revoke photo access: iOS Settings → ZapLog → Photos → Off
  • Export data: Settings → Export CSV
  • View data: Direct access from the main App interface

7. Children's Privacy

ZapLog is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Policy Updates

If we make significant changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you prominently within the App. Please review this page periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: zaplogapp@icloud.com
  • App Store Feedback: TestFlight or App Store review page

10. Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with the following regulations:

  • China Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL)
  • China Cybersecurity Law
  • GDPR (EU General Data Protection Regulation, if you are located in the EU)

ZapLog — Privacy First. Data Never Leaves Your Device.
