常见问题

ZapLog 是什么？
ZapLog（无感记账）是一款 iOS 应用，通过 OCR 技术自动识别支付宝、微信支付、京东支付等截图中金额，自动完成记账，无需手动输入。
我的数据安全吗？
数据完全存储在您的本地设备，不会上传至任何服务器。删除 App 后数据一并清除。
自动删除功能是如何工作的？
开启后，成功识别的截图会从系统相册移至「最近删除」，可在相册中手动恢复，30 天后自动清除。
识别失败怎么办？
请确保截图包含完整的支付完成页面，金额、商户、时间信息清晰可见。裁剪或模糊的截图可能影响识别准确性。

ZapLog · Expense Tracker Support

Thank you for using ZapLog. Below are common questions and contact information.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ZapLog?
ZapLog is an iOS app that uses OCR to automatically detect and parse expense amounts from Alipay, WeChat Pay, JD Pay, and other payment screenshots — no manual entry needed.
Is my data safe?
All data is stored locally on your device only. Nothing is sent to any server. Uninstalling the app removes all data permanently.
How does auto-delete work?
When enabled, recognized screenshots are moved to Recently Deleted in your photo library. You can manually recover them within 30 days before they are permanently removed.
What if recognition fails?
Make sure the screenshot includes the full payment confirmation screen with clearly visible amount, merchant name, and timestamp. Cropped or blurry screenshots may reduce accuracy.
